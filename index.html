The uDacity Frogger Game

Use the arrow keys to cross the path to the water and collect the stars without getting hit by a bug or you lose a turn.

You gain a point for every star you collect. How many can you collect before the game is over?

Gems will travel across the path just like bugs but if you can catch them, you get extra turns.

Click on the hamburger icon to change your character or your level and to reset the game.
